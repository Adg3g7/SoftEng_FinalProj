Meet the Bad Guys


Captain Allergy

This big bad is a hot-headed male plant biologist that likes to spread his spores. He is bitter about the numerous food allergies he had as a kid and now is taking over campus with his powerful plant technology.

Favorite color: Green (like plants!!)
Favorite food: Salad
Spirit Animal: Grasshopper
Favorite Movie: Little Shop of Horrors

Captain Allergy uses his powers to wreak havoc across the campus.

Vincent

This sulky boy is a power obsessed male that sucks the energy of the people around him. He plans to take over the campus with his negative attitude. He is Captain Allergy's evil partner. He real villain name is Vincentamin Deficientcy as his grand plan is to take away all nutrients from campus.

Favorite color: Gray
Favorite food: Oranges
Spirit Animal: Raccoon
Favorite Movie: Silence of the Lambs

Vincent can only be defeated through wit and strategy.
